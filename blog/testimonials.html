How to Get Testimonials That Convert

Social proof that sells · Updated June 2026 · Scripts included

Testimonials are your #1 conversion tool. Most freelancers collect weak testimonials. Here's how to get powerful ones.

🎯 The Perfect Testimonial Formula

A great testimonial answers these 4 questions:

  1. Before: What was the problem?
  2. During: What was working with you like?
  3. After: What was the result?
  4. Would you recommend? To whom?

✅ Great Testimonial

"Before working with Sarah, our website was loading in 8 seconds and we were losing 40% of visitors. Sarah was incredibly responsive — she gave us daily updates and asked smart questions we hadn't considered. Within 3 weeks, our load time dropped to 2 seconds and conversions increased 35%. I recommend Sarah to any SaaS company that cares about performance."

— John Smith, CEO at Acme Corp

❌ Weak Testimonial

"Sarah was great to work with. Highly recommend!"

— John Smith

📧 When to Ask for Testimonials

  • Right after delivery: When they're excited about the result
  • After they see ROI: When the numbers come in
  • After a thank-you: If they say "thank you," immediately ask
  • Not during the project: They haven't seen the result yet
  • Not when they're stressed: Bad timing = weak testimonial

📝 The Testimonial Request Script

Email Subject: Quick favor? (2 minutes)

Hi [Name],

I'm so glad [project] is working well for you! I have a quick favor to ask.

Would you be willing to share a brief testimonial about working with me? I'm looking for something that covers:

• What challenge you were facing before we started
• What the process was like working together
• What result you achieved (with numbers if possible)
• Whether you'd recommend me to others

Just 2-3 sentences is perfect. You can reply directly to this email.

Thank you so much!

[Your Name]

💡 Pro tip: Make it easy. If they don't reply in 3 days, send a follow-up with a draft they can edit: "Here's a quick draft based on what you told me. Feel free to edit or rewrite entirely!"

🎥 Video Testimonials

Video testimonials are 10x more powerful than text. Here's how to get them:

  • Ask during a call: "Mind if I record you saying that? It would be huge for my business."
  • Send a Loom link: "Could you record a 30-second video on your phone? Just 3 questions: What was the problem? What was working with me like? What was the result?"
  • Offer to draft it: "I can write a script and you can record it on your phone. Takes 2 minutes."

📊 Where to Use Testimonials

  • Homepage: 3 best testimonials above the fold
  • Portfolio: One testimonial per case study
  • Proposals: Relevant testimonial for similar projects
  • LinkedIn: Ask for LinkedIn recommendations too
  • Social media: Screenshot great testimonials and share
  • Email signature: One short testimonial at the bottom

🔄 The Testimonial Flywheel

Great work → Happy client → Ask for testimonial → Use in marketing → Get more clients → Great work → ...

The key is asking. Most freelancers never ask. The ones who do ask get 3-5x more testimonials.
